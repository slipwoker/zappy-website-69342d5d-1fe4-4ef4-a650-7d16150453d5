How do I request a game?

We're always looking to expand our unblocked games website library. If there's a specific title you'd like to see, drop us a message through our contact form with the game name and why you think it'd be a great addition. We review all requests and prioritize based on demand and compatibility with our platform.

Why might a game be unavailable?

Some games may be temporarily down for maintenance, or certain titles might have licensing restrictions that prevent us from hosting them. We work hard to keep our free online games collection running smoothly, but occasional technical issues can arise. If a game you love isn't working, let us know so we can investigate.

Do you support mobile?

Yes! Most games on GameVault are optimized for mobile browsers, so you can play on your phone or tablet. However, some titles work best on desktop due to control schemes or performance requirements. We're continuously improving mobile compatibility across our entire catalog.

What should I do if a game doesn't load?

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If that doesn't work, check your internet connection or try a different browser. Some school or workplace networks may block certain content, which can affect gameplay. If the issue persists, reach out to us with details about the game and your device.

How do you choose what to include?

We curate our collection based on player feedback, trending titles, and games that run reliably in browser environments. Our goal is to offer a diverse mix of genres and styles that appeal to gamers of all interests, while ensuring every game meets our quality and performance standards.
